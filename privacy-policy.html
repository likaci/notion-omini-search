Privacy Policy for Notion Omni Search

Last updated: August 2, 2025

Summary: Notion Omni Search does not collect any user data. The extension operates entirely locally within your browser.

Data Collection

Notion Omni Search does not collect any user data. The extension:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not track your browsing history
  • Does not store any search queries
  • Does not transmit data to external servers
  • Does not use cookies or local storage

How the Extension Works

The extension functions by:

  • Reading search keywords from URL parameters when you navigate to Notion
  • Automatically triggering Notion's built-in search functionality
  • Operating entirely within your browser's sandbox
  • Not requiring any external network requests

Permissions

The extension requires minimal permissions:

  • activeTab: To interact with Notion pages when you visit them
  • Host permission for notion.so: To run content scripts on Notion pages

These permissions are essential for the extension's core functionality and are not used for data collection.

Third-Party Services

Notion Omni Search does not integrate with any third-party services or APIs. All functionality is self-contained within the extension.

Data Retention

Since no user data is collected, there is no data to retain or delete.

Children's Privacy

Notion Omni Search does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13, as no personal information is collected from any users.
